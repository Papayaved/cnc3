Work panel

Buttons

  • Home - go to the Home panel

    The button is disabled during running.

  • Break

    Wire break control turn On/Off.

  • Pump

    Cutting fluid turn On/Off.

  • Roll

    Wire roll turn On/Off.

  • Start/Stop

    Start - begin or continue cutting;
    Stop - stop cutting.

  • Back - return back along the cutting contour

    This button is active after pressing the "Stop" button.

    M and G-codes that control the operation modes are ignored when CNC is reading the program in the reverse direction.

  • Cancel - cancel cutting

    The button is active after pressing the "Stop" button.

    By pressing this button, the current state of the CNC is saved and the cut is turned off. After turning off the power, you can resume work from the same position using the "Recovery" button on the Home panel.

  • Hold - motors' holding turn On/Off

    Enable/disable motor drivers. When the motor drivers are enabled, the motors are holding the workpiece otherwise you can move it manually.

Controls

  • Roll - wire roll velocity

    Values from 1 to 7.

  • Speed - maximum cutting speed

    Values from 0,01 to 18 mm/min.

Flag

  • Idle run

    Enable/disable idle mode. In idle mode, the CNC is allowed to work in modes that are not compatible with cutting a workpiece. The idle mode is used for tuning purposes.

State panel

The State panel displays:

  • Current coordinates X, Y, U, V in millimeters;;
  • Elapsed time from the start in HH:MM:SS format;
  • Estimated remaining time in HH:MM:SS format;
  • Flags:
    • PWR - no power supply 220V;
    • WB - wire break signal;
    • FB - feedback limit switch ((XU3:4));
    • ALM - an alarm signal from one of the limit switches ((XU3:1));
    • REV - the limit switch of the backward motion of the wire roll is active (XU3:3);
    • FWD - the limit switch of the forward motion of the wire roll is active (XU3:2).
