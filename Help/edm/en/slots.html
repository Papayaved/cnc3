Slots Panel

Parameters

  • Wire Diameter

  • Slots Depth

    Signed value.

  • Step

    The thickness of the parts. Signed value. The sign depends on the processing direction.

  • Slots Number

  • Cutting Speed

    Speed in the forward direction.

  • Roll Velocity

    Wire roll velocity when the CNC is cutting in the forward direction.

  • Idle Speed

    Speed in the reverse direction.

  • Idle Roll Velocity

    Wire roll velocity when the CNC is moving in the reverse direction.

  • Pause

    Pause time after cutting in the forward direction. This parameter is used when the wire bends due to excessive cutting speed.

  • Axis

    Axis of the processing direction. The direction also depends on the sign of the step of the slots.

    • X

    • Y

Buttons

  • Reset

    Reset to default.

  • Undo

    Return last used.

  • Generate

    Generate G-code.
