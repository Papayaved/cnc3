Centering

Bottom panel

Buttons

  • Home - go to the Home panel

    The button is disabled during running.

  • X+

    Search for the workpiece edge in the direction of increasing the X-coordinate

  • X-

    Search for the workpiece edge in the direction of decreasing the X-coordinate.

  • Y+

    Search for the workpiece edge in the direction of increasing the Y-coordinate.

  • Y-

    Search for the workpiece edge in the direction of decreasing the Y-coordinate.

  • Center

    Search the center of the round hole.

  • Weak Voltage

    Weak High voltage turn On/Off.

  • Hold

    Motors' holding turn On/Off.

  • Cancel

    Cancel moving.

State panel

  • Speed

    Current workpiece speed.

  • Voltage

    Current voltage between wire and workpiece.

  • X, Y

    Current coordinates.

  • Flags:

    • PWR - no power supply 220V;
    • WB - wire break signal;
    • TO - short circuit timeout;
    • FB - feedback limit switch;
    • ALM - an alarm signal from one of the limit switches;
    • REV - the limit switch of the backward motion of the wire roll is active;
    • FWD - the limit switch of the forward motion of the wire roll is active.

Task panel

  • Mode

    • Edge search - search the edge of a workpiece;
    • 4-point search - search the center of a round hole in a workpiece;
    • 3-point search - search the center of the arc at the workpiece edge.

  • Search radius

    Maximum search distance.

  • Fine zone

    The size of a slow motion zone. This zone is used for precision search the edge of the workpiece

  • Rollback

    Rollback distance after the workpiece edge is found.

  • Coarse speed

    Initial speed.

  • Fine speed

    Fine search speed.

  • Roll velocity

    Roll velocity, from 0 to 7. 0 corresponds to an unmoved wire.

  • Threshold

    Touch voltage.

  • Touches

    The number of touches when CNC is searching for the workpiece edge.

  • Attempts

    The number of attempts to find the center.

  • Angle 1, 2, 3

    Set search angles for 3-point mode.

  • Reset position

    Same as
    G92 X0 Y0
