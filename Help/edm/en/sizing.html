Size measuring for a 4-axle machine

This panel is an instrument for accurately measuring the position of the machine bed and the distance between the rollers. To do this, the CNC will cut a truncated cone, after which it will be necessary to measure the top and the bottom diameters. For better measurement accuracy, use a thick workpiece.

  • Wire Diameter

  • Maximum U, V motors travel (UV_MAX)

    Maximum motor travel along the U and V axes.

  • Distance between rollers (L)

    Distance between axes of rollers.

  • Workpiece bottom height (H)

    Distance between the axis of the lower roller and the bottom of the workpiece.

  • Workpiece thickness (T)

    The workpiece must have a rectangular profile.

  • Roller diameter

    The inner diameter on which the wire lies, not the outer.

  • Roller plane

    • XZ

      Rollers are placed along the X-axis.

    • YZ

      Rollers are placed along the Y-axis.

  • Entry line length

    The length of the Entry Line for a truncated cone.

  • Cutting direction

    • X+

      Cut a truncated cone in the direction of increasing the X-axis.

    • X-

      Cut a truncated cone in the direction of decreasing the X-axis.

    • Y+

      Cut a truncated cone in the direction of increasing the Y-axis.

    • Y-

      Cut a truncated cone in the direction of decreasing the Y-axis.

  • Button Generate

    Generate a G-code for a truncated cone.

Recalculate

Measure the top and the bottom diameters of the cut truncated cone.

  • Measured top diameter

  • Measured bottom diameter

  • Button Recalculate

    Press to calculate machine parameters.

Result

  • L

    The actual distance between rollers.

  • H

    The actual height of the bottom of the workpiece (machine bed) above the axis of the bottom roller.
